Greetings!

A native of Vienna, Austria, I waltzed my way to New York in 2005. I work as an animator, video designer and illustrator in theater, film and installation. My animation work draws on my interest and background in theater, puppetry and opera.

I’ve been lead animator on a host of Broadway shows, including Motown: the Musical, Jekyll & Hyde, Bring it On, and Shakespeare’s Macbeth at Lincoln Center Theater. I’ve also done animation work for Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat, the National Tour, as well as Ringling Brothers’ LEGENDS Tour and Disney’s Aladdin on Broadway.

International credits include Cinderella for the Dutch National Ballet in Amsterdam, and A Winter’s Tale for the Royal Ballet at Covent Garden.

In addition to theater I’ve done stage graphics and and internet branding for various festivals including Bonnaroo, Googa Mooga, and Kanye West’s set at Lollapalooza, Santiago.

In New York I’ve worked, among other studios, at Transistor, MTV, Sesame Workshop, Little Airplane Productions, and in London with the BBC and Blink Productions.
