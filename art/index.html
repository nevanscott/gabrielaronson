New York Musical Theatre 2010 Graphic Identity
As the lead designer for the New York Musical Theatre Festival, I designed practically all the print and video materials of the 2010 Festival. This included programs, tickets, one-sheet posters, business cards, etc. In addition I designed streetbanners that lined 42nd street and 8th Avenue, and animated several jumbotrons for Times Square including the 200 foot tall American Eagle array.
